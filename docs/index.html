Introduction to BUT Informatique

The BUT Informatique (IT) have a duration of 3 years, it trains us in the design, production and implementation of IT solutions that meet the digital transformation needs of organisations. You don't need any skill in programming to enter this course in BUT.

This training aims to develop six essential skills for the student of the B.U.T. Computer science :

  1. Perform application development
  2. Optimize computer applications
  3. Organise a complex communicating computer systems
  4. Manage information data
  5. Conduct a project
  6. Work in an IT team

There are 4 Courses (Parcours) available :

Data Administration, Management & Exploitation The Administration, management and use of data course brings together knowledge to administer data warehouses and visualize the latter to develop decision-making applications.
This path focuses on the last 3 skills of the program and less on the first 3.
Deployment Of Communicating & Secure Applications The Deployment of communicating and secure applications course is based around two axes: installation, system configuration operations, the deployment and securing of networks on the one hand, the installation and configuration of application services as well as the development of client-server applications meeting the requirements of an organization on the other hand.
This path focuses on skills 1, 3 and 6 of the program and places less emphasis on skills 2, 4 and 5.
Integration of Applications & Management of the Information System The course Integration of applications and management of the information system brings together skills related to the development, integration of applications and support functions for the management of digital transformation projects for organizations.
This path focuses on skills 1, 5 and 6 of the program and less on skills 2, 3 and 4.
Realisation of Applications: Design, Development, Validation The course Creating applications: design, development, validation focuses on the software life cycle: from the expression of the needs of the customer, design, programming, validation and application maintenance.
This path focuses on skills 1, 2 and 6 of the program and less focused into skills 3, 4 and 5.
