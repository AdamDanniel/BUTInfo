Student Life

Time Table

    BUT lessons takes place as follows:

  • Cours Magistraux (CM)

    Lectures in Amphitheater

  • Travaux Dirigés (TD)

    Numerous tutorials to put into practice the knowledge seen in class. Generally doing practices and exercises. (not using computer)

  • Travaux Pratiques (TP)

    Practical works or exercises that allows us to perform anything practically. (with computer)

  • Situation D'apprentissage et D'évaluation (SAÉ)

    An assignment consisting of one or more tasks to be carried out by the student in order to achieve the set goal. It could exist in an individual assignment or a group assignment

  • Example of Weekly Timetable:

    Average of 31 hours of classes per week:
    20% Cours Magistraux (CM)
    15% Travaux Dirigés (TD)
    10% SAÉ
    55% Travaux Pratiques (TP)

Exams

  • Contrôle Continue

    Sets of exercises to test our knowledge about what we just learn to help us keep up with all of the silibus.
    Carries a small coefficient to our marks.

  • TP / TD Noté

    Practical works or tutorials that has marks.
    Carries a medium amount of coefficient to our marks.

  • Question Choix Multiple (QCM)

    Objective sets of questions with multiple answer choices generally.
    Carries a small coefficient to our marks.

  • Devoir Surveillé

    Exams on paper with a larger amount of time to do it.
    Carries a big coefficient to our marks.
